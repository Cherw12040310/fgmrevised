HOW CAN YOU HELP?


A. Share this page on social media such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter to spread the word about the millions of women and girls at-risk of female genital mutilation.

B. Support and donate to the high-impact, grassroots nonprofit organizations that are working to end FGM below

Hope for Girls & Women

Their mission offer safe house residents free classes in vocational training. Through this training, women can become skillful and making them less vulnerable to forced marriage and FGM.



Rights of Girls

Their mission is to provide a valuable farm animal, a goat, to African village families to aid in the prevention of FGM



Beyond FGM

Beyond FGM is a charitable orgranization that comprises of a team of midwives and companions from the UK who collaborated to put an end to FGM in the Pokot community. Their initiative for this project is to worked as a midwifery tutor at a nursing school in Pokot and observed the prevalence of FGM.



Orchid Project

Orchid Project is a UK-based NGO that is catalysing the global movement to end female genital cutting (FGC).
